# Public Edition Notice

Sovereign System Architecture is published as a Public Edition for visibility, documentation, and portfolio review.

The Public Edition is intentionally limited. It is not a full enterprise transfer package and should not be interpreted as a complete production implementation.

## Included in the Public Edition

The Public Edition may include:

- Static showcase files.
- Public README and governance documentation.
- High-level positioning for sovereign AI, zero-trust architecture, and defensive security interface concepts.
- Public support, contribution, security, and licensing boundaries.

## Excluded from the Public Edition

The Public Edition excludes:

- Private source logic and proprietary implementation details.
- Customer data, client environments, and deployment assumptions.
- Operational deception logic, detection rules, telemetry pipelines, or monitoring schemas.
- Internal prompts, private playbooks, response procedures, or business strategy.
- API keys, tokens, credentials, secrets, or private configuration.
- Commercial contracts, acquisition materials, pricing, and enterprise transfer records.

## Private Enterprise Transfer Package

A Private Enterprise Transfer Package may be prepared separately for qualified commercial, acquisition, or diligence conversations.

That package may include private materials under written agreement only. Nothing in the Public Edition grants access to those materials or creates a right to use them.

## Procurement-Safe Interpretation

The Public Edition is suitable for initial review of positioning and governance posture. Any production, compliance, security, transfer, or acquisition assessment should be performed against the applicable private package and written commercial terms.
